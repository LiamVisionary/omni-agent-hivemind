What are emojis?

Standardized pictographic characters that help text sound more human.

🙂

Picture characters

Unlike old-school emoticons such as :-), emojis are Unicode characters that apps can render as colorful little pictures.

🌐

A shared language

Unicode keeps core meanings portable, while Apple, Google, Microsoft, and others give each emoji its own visual flavor.

Why people use them

They make short messages clearer, warmer, faster, and a little more fun.
